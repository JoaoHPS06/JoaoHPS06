### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Computer Science undergraduate** (7th semester) at **UFOP – Federal University of Ouro Preto**, Brazil, working at the intersection of **AI** and **immersive technology**.

- 🔭 Currently researching **AR educational systems** using ARCore + Unity 3D at UFOP's Pharmacy Museum
- 🤖 Building **LLM-powered applications** with Anthropic and Gemini APIs
- 🏗️ Passionate about **distributed systems**, backend engineering and AI
- 🌎 Fluent in **English**, intermediate **Spanish**
- 📬 Open to **junior roles and internships** in AI Engineering, Backend or XR

### 🔬 Active Research

**Augmented Reality for Cultural Heritage — UFOP Pharmacy Museum**
> ARCore + Unity 3D system that overlays interactive historical content onto physical artifacts in a 19th-century pharmacy museum. Focused on spatial computing, marker-based tracking and educational UX design for immersive environments.
